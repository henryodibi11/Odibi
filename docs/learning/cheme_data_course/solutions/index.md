## How to Use Solutions

1. **Try the exercises first** - Don't peek immediately!
2. **Compare your approach** - Solutions show one way, not the only way
3. **Run the YAML** - All solution files are in `/examples/cheme_course/`
4. **Ask yourself:** "Why did they do it this way? What's different from mine?"

---

*Solutions are meant to reinforce learning, not replace doing the work.*
