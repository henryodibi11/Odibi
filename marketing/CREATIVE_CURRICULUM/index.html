    Creative Skills Curriculum

    A 20-week mastery plan built around 3 comprehensive courses. ~10 hours/week.

    Philosophy

    Deep Learning: 3 extensive courses to build real, lasting skills. Parallel Tracks: Overlap courses to keep variety and momentum. Weekly Projects: Apply what you learn immediately. Master the Tools: By the end, you'll be genuinely proficient—not just "familiar."

    The 3 Core Courses

    # Course Platform Hours Focus
    1 Logo Design in Adobe Illustrator Udemy 17h Logo design, Illustrator mastery, brand identity
    2 Premiere Pro Masterclass: Video Editing Udemy 27h Complete video editing, color, audio, effects
    3 After Effects: Motion Graphics Masterclass Udemy 16h Motion graphics, animation, visual effects

    Total Course Time: ~60 hours Total with Projects: ~200 hours over 20 weeks

    Tips for Success

    1. Watch at 1.25x-1.5x speed if you're comfortable
    2. Take notes on techniques you want to remember
    3. Don't skip projects—they're where real learning happens
    4. Save inspiration (reels, motion graphics, styles you like)
    5. Post imperfect work—feedback accelerates learning
    6. Revisit difficult sections—mastery requires repetition
    7. Build a reference library of your favorite effects and techniques
