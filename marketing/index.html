    Odibi Marketing Campaign

    A 6-month LinkedIn + Medium content campaign to build visibility for Odibi and establish expertise.

    Campaign Overview

    Metric Value
    Duration 26 weeks (6 months)
    LinkedIn Posts 78 total (3/week)
    Medium Articles ~24 total (~1/week)
    Start Date Your choice
    End Goal Credibility, job opportunities, Odibi users

    Campaign Files

    LinkedIn Posts (Copy-Paste Ready)

    File Phase Weeks Posts
    campaign_phase1_credibility.md Credibility Building 1-2 6
    campaign_phase2_building_in_public.md Building in Public 3-10 24
    campaign_phase3_pattern_deep_dives.md Pattern Deep Dives 11-16 18
    campaign_phase4_antipatterns.md Anti-Patterns 17-22 18
    campaign_phase5_advanced_community.md Advanced & Community 23-26 12

    Medium Articles (Full Content)

    File Topic
    articles/article_01_origin_story.md Your journey from operations to data engineering
    articles/article_02_bronze_layer_mistake.md Why never to transform in Bronze
    articles/article_03_bronze_layer_setup.md Complete Bronze layer walkthrough
    articles/article_10_introducing_odibi.md Odibi launch article
    medium_articles.md Outlines for remaining articles

    More full articles can be generated as needed.

    Quick Start

    1. Week 1: Start with Phase 1, Post 1.1 (Your Origin Story)
    2. Post 3x/week: Monday, Wednesday, Friday works well
    3. Time: 8-10am your timezone for best reach
    4. Engage: Respond to every comment within 24 hours

    Content Strategy

    Phase 1: Don't Mention Odibi

    Build credibility first. Share your story, lessons learned. No product pitch.

    Phase 2: Introduce Odibi Gradually

    By week 10, reveal that you've been using Odibi for the series.

    Phase 3-4: Establish Expertise

    Deep technical content. Show you know your stuff.

    Phase 5: Community Building

    Invite collaboration, thank followers, call to action.

    Tone Guidelines

    ✅ Do: - Share real mistakes and lessons - Use "I" not "we" (personal brand) - Be humble: "I'm still learning" - Ask questions to drive engagement - Use specific numbers and examples

    ❌ Don't: - Mention your company by name - Complain about specific people - Be preachy or lecture-y - Use corporate speak - Oversell Odibi

    Dataset

    The series uses the Brazilian E-Commerce dataset from Kaggle.

    Download and set it up before Week 3.

    Tracking Success

    Track these weekly: - Post impressions - Engagement (likes, comments) - Profile views - Connection requests - Inbound messages

    Adjust based on what resonates.

    Timeline Example

    If you start January 1st:

    Month Weeks Phase Focus
    January 1-4 1-2 Credibility + Bronze/Silver
    February 5-8 2 Silver/Gold + Dimensions
    March 9-13 2-3 Odibi reveal + Patterns
    April 14-17 3-4 Patterns + Anti-patterns
    May 18-22 4 Anti-patterns + Debugging
    June 23-26 5 Advanced + Community

    After the Campaign

    • Continue posting 1-2x/week
    • Focus on what got engagement
    • Respond to community questions
    • Keep improving Odibi based on feedback
    • Convert visibility into opportunities
